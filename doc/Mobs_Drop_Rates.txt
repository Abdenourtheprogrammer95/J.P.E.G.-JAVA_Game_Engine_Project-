*** Bone: (FIX)
Skeletons, wither skeletons, skeleton horses, and strays may drop 0–2 bones upon death.
The maximum drop is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 0-5 bones
with Looting III.

*** String: (FIX)
Spiders and cave spiders drop 0–2 string upon death. The maximum is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 0–5 string with Looting III.

*** Arrow: (FIX)
Skeletons and strays drop 0-2 arrows upon death. The maximum drop is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 0–5 arrows with Looting III.

*** Rotten_Flesh: (FIX)
Zombies drop from 0 to 2 units of rotten flesh. Looting can increase this by one per
level, for a maximum of 5 rotten flesh.

*** Potato, Carrot, Iron_Ingot:
Zombies have a 2.5% chance of dropping either an iron ingot, carrot, or potato upon death. This is increased by 1% per level of looting, giving each of them the following chances:

• 0.83%
• 1.17% with Looting I
• 1.50% with Looting II
• 1.83% with Looting III

*** Phantom_Membrane: (FIX)
Phantoms drop 0–1 phantom membrane when killed by a player or tamed wolf. Looting
increases the maximum drops by 1 per level, for a maximum of 4 with Looting III.

*** Spider_Eye:
Spiders have a 1⁄3 chance of dropping a spider eye. The maximum amount of spider eyes
is increased by 1 per level of Looting. The chance of a spider not dropping any spider eyes can be found using the formula 2 / (Looting Level + 3). All other amounts have an equal chance of occurring.
